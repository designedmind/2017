Keynote Speakers

 

Daniel C. Dennett
Tufts University

Author of Consciousness Explained (Little, Brown and Co., 1991), Freedom Evolves (Viking Penguin, 2003) and Darwin's Dangerous Idea (Simon & Schuster, 1995), Daniel Dennett is University Professor and Austin B. Fletcher Professor of Philosophy, and Co-Director of the Center for Cognitive Studies at Tufts University.

 
 

Michael Graziano
Princeton University

Author of Consciousness and the Social Brain (Oxford University Press, 2013), Michael Graziano is Professor of Psychology and Neuroscience at Princeton University.
