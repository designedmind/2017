Symposium on the science of consciousness

DESIGNED MIND SYMPOSIUM

University of Edinburgh · 22 & 23 November 2017

Our intuition tells us that consciousness is a special non-physical realm, inhabited by thoughts and feelings. An emerging scientific picture reveals consciousness as an elaborate form of behaviour, designed by evolution to give us powerful reflective capabilities that non-conscious beings lack. A rich internal narrative generates that behaviour: a virtual world which ascribes phenomenal properties — colours, tastes, feelings — to things and to ourselves.

This two-day event brings together researchers working on all aspects of the science of consciousness, including experimental psychology, neuroscience, philosophy of mind and artificial consciousness.
